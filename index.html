CHAIRMAN MESSAGE

|who we are

On behalf of Advance Group of Companies (AGC), I would like to thank you for visiting our website. As I look at the growth over the years since our inception in 1997, I am extremely proud of what we have achieved, and even more excited about our outlook for an equally promising future. The story of Advance Group of Companies (AGC) told through our clients, our employees ... and the communities in which we work. Our history can be traced back to one modest yet inspired engineering office located in Karachi, where the first company of (AGC) Advance Engineering Associates (AEA) a consulting Engineering firm first laid down its technical service roots. Since those early days, AEA has stayed true to what we have always believed in – that our clients, employees deserve our best, and this is still the foundation for our dynamic domestic and global operations today. R & R Engineering Associates is a leading Engineering Procurement, Supplier and Construction Company for Electrical, Mechanical and Renewable energy solutions (Solar PV and Wind Turbine) for Industrial, commercial and domestic, with optimum mix of quality and timeliness, thereby giving maximum value to the customers. Established in 2004, R & R Engineering Associates has been supplying high quality power solutions into various market sectors building a long history of satisfied customers. R & R Engineering Associates is a part of Advance Group of Companies (AGC). Advance Group of Companies also consists of Herts Renewable Energy Solutions LTD with is based in UK and R & R Engineering Associates, which is based in Karachi, Pakistan. We are confident about the future of Advance Engineering Associates (AGC) as we look toward new opportunities and continued growth in our many fields of service in Pakistan, UK and UAE. I encourage you to contact us to learn more about the services. On behalf Advance Group of Companies (AGC), thank you for taking the time to learn more about us.
